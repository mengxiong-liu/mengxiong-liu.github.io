Mengxiong Liu
mengxiol@alumni.cmu.edu

Mengxiong currently serves as a research scientist at 1X Technologies, an eminent firm specializing in the advancement of general-purpose humanoid robots and supported by OpenAI. In addition to his role at 1X Technologies, he is an active angel investor in private market investments. Mengxiong is a proponent of the transformative potential of general-purpose humanoid robots, which he believes could revolutionize our lives and work environments in a manner akin to the impact of the Industrial Revolution.

Before his tenure with humanoid robots, Mengxiong led the development of behavior and motion planning at Nuro, an industry-leading autonomous vehicle company with a valuation of $8.6 billion, and backed by SoftBank.
